DEAR OPEN SOURCE ENTHUSIASTS,

We are excited to announce Opencode, an annual open sourced development event organized by Geekhaven, the technical society of Indian Institute of Information Technology Allahabad (IIIT Allahabad).

Opencode is a month-long event that provides a platform for individuals and teams to showcase their skills and creativity in coding, designing, and building solutions to tackle real-world problems. This event offers a unique opportunity for developers and enthusiasts to come together and collaborate on exciting open source projects.

In addition to the opportunity to work on cutting-edge projects, Opencode offers numerous other benefits. Participants will have the chance to network with other like-minded individuals, learn from experts in the field, and gain valuable experience that can help boost their careers.

Opencode also offers a competitive element, with a leaderboard that tracks participants' progress and achievements. The top performers will have the chance to win exciting prizes and recognition for their contributions. We believe that Opencode has something to offer everyone, regardless of their level of experience or expertise. So, whether you are a seasoned developer or just starting out, we encourage you to take part in this exciting event.

To learn more and to register for Opencode, please visit our website at here. We look forward to seeing you there!
